## Source Story

### Story 1.6: Generated Drift Validation

As a maintainer,
I want validation to detect stale generated files,
So that committed generated artifacts remain reproducible and are not hand-edited as final fixes.

**Acceptance Criteria:**

**Given** the Story 1.5 generator contract artifacts are committed, including generator entry-point scripts, `docs/generator-contracts.md`, schema fixtures, and any skeleton outputs generated by `make generate`
**When** `make validate` runs
**Then** it runs metadata validation, tag validation, generation, and generated-file drift checks for Epic 1 scope.
**And** validation fails if those Story 1.5 contract artifacts or skeleton outputs are stale.
**And** `make generate` followed by `make validate` is clean on a fresh checkout.
**And** drift failure messages identify the stale artifact and the command to regenerate it.
**And** drift validation is deterministic and does not depend on local timezone.
**And** full Dockerfile, Bake, matrix, catalog, publish digest, and docs drift validation are extended by the later stories that own those artifacts.
